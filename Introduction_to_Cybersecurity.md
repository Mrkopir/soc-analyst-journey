# Introduction to Cybersecurity - TryHackMe

## 1. Basic concepts

### 1.1 Offensive vs Defensive Security:

- **Offensive Security**
    - Goal: Attack simulation, finding vulnerabilities
    - **examples of careears:**
        - Penetration Tester
        - Red Teamer 
    - **purpose:** Think like hacker to improve system
- **Defensive Security**
  - Goal: Protect and monitor systems
  - **examples of careears:**
    - Blue Teaming
    - Digital Forensics
    - Incident Response
  - **purpose:** Prevent attacks and respond to incidents quickly

### 1.2 Key Security Principles:
- **CIA OR ЦКД**:
    - **Confidentiality** – keeping data secret
    - **Integrity** – ensuring data is accurate and untampered
    - **Availability** – ensuring systems are accessible
